# Event header earth background — design

## Purpose

Create a wide background texture for the AfroPasso dance-event header. The asset should support light overlaid interface text while reinforcing the site's warm cultural-documentary direction.

## Visual direction

Use a hybrid documentary-and-painterly treatment: realistic cracked, sun-baked red-ochre earth softened by matte editorial grain. Fine branching root lines should wander organically through the surface and suggest migration or lineage without forming a literal map, diagram, symbol, border, or decorative cultural pattern.

The palette stays within ochre, terracotta, clay, aged parchment, and warm brown. The lower part of the image becomes naturally darker and quieter so light text remains readable. The middle should avoid high-contrast focal details that would compete with a header title.

## Composition and output

- Landscape ratio: 3:1.
- Target dimensions: approximately 1920 × 640 px.
- Final format: WebP, encoded with `cwebp -q 92 -m 6`.
- Final project path: `assets/events/event-header-earth.webp`.
- The full surface must work with cover-style cropping.

## Constraints

- No people, text, typography, logos, watermarks, icons, or explicit symbols.
- No neon, nightclub aesthetics, saturated synthetic colors, or glossy 3D rendering.
- No exoticizing imagery or arbitrary decorative cultural motifs.
- Root lines remain subtle, fine, irregular, and embedded in the earth texture.
- Overall mood: tactile, editorial, timeless, respectful, mid-to-dark.

## Acceptance checks

- The source image follows the approved 3:1 horizontal composition.
- The lower edge is visibly darker but does not resemble an artificial gradient overlay.
- Light header text can remain legible over the lower region.
- The result contains no forbidden visual elements.
- The final WebP exists at the specified path and can be decoded successfully.
